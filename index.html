The harmonic constants, 2025

With Distributed Gallery

Each artwork on this map is an NFT whose visibility and availability are directly tied to the Thames tide. As the tide recedes, the work gradually appears; as the tide rises, it fades. Each NFT is only tradable for one hour at low tide, correlating its liquidity to the natural rhythm of the river. This tidal protocol will remain active for the next twelve years.

The locking and unlocking of each artwork is handled by the [aside] protocol running on Ethereum. The tidal data that governs each artwork's state is embedded directly into its metadata and stored permanently via IPFS.

This project is a way to enact one of [aside·eth]'s founding intuitions: to act as the stitching point between phenomena that are ordinarily separated, by linking artworks' liquidity to outside world events through oracle technologies. Thus, the economic lifecycle of an artwork and the world's life become truly interdependent.

Process & Research

The tidal cycles inform both the physical and digital artwork, creating a rhythmic pattern that echoes the natural flows of the Thames river itself.
